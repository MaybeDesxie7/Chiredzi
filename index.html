About Chiredzi Government High School

Founded to serve Chiredzi and neighbouring communities, we offer a balanced education that blends academics, vocational skills, sports and arts. Our mission is to produce well-rounded citizens prepared for higher education and meaningful work.

Vision & Mission

To develop knowledgeable, ethical and resourceful young people who contribute positively to society.

  • Inclusive learning — accessible programs for all learners
  • Practical skills — labs & workshops
  • Community engagement — service & leadership

Director's Message

“We welcome all learners and aim to provide a safe and inspiring environment for growth. Join us in building a brighter future.” — Mrs. A. Chivasa, Headmistress
