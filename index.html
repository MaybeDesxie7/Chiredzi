About Chiredzi Government High School

We are a forward-looking institution committed to academic excellence, ethical leadership and whole-person education. Our programs blend rigorous academics with co-curricular opportunities so learners thrive in and out of the classroom.

Our mission: To cultivate confident, compassionate and capable leaders through excellent teaching and strong values.
